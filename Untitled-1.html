Grandma's lemon maringue pie


    one 9-inch pie
    30 min-prep time
    8 servings

INGREDIENTS

    1 cup white sugar
    2tbsp>/all-purpose flour
      3 tbsp cornstarch
      1/4 tsp cornstarch
      1 1/2 cups water
      2 lemons, juiced and zested
      2 tbsp butter
      4 egg yorks,beaten
      1 (9 inch)pie crust,baked
      4 egg whites sugar
    6 tbsp white sugar
